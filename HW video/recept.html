Spaghetti

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large pan, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and Parmesan cheese together. Add a pinch of black pepper.
  4. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the pancetta in the pan. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix the egg and cheese mixture into the pasta, stirring vigorously to coat the spaghetti without scrambling the eggs. Add pasta water if needed for a creamy texture.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley if desired. Serve immediately.
